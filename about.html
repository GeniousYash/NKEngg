Dies & Tools

NK Engg Dies & Tools is a premier manufacturer specializing in plastic injection molds, rubber injection molds, and custom plastic components. Our state-of-the-art technology, combined with a team of highly skilled designers and engineers, ensures that we deliver products that meet the highest standards of quality and precision.

We are committed to exceeding customer expectations by providing innovative and efficient solutions tailored to specific requirements.

We have been carrying on this business since 2003. We are specialized in Plastic Injection Mold , Blow Molds , Rubber Molds , Jigs and Fixtures. & Plastic Component.

We specialize in the manufacturing of molds and molded parts, offering comprehensive solutions for your plastic product needs. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped to produce medium-sized molds suitable for molding machines ranging from 50 tons to 550 tons, with a mold-making capacity of up to 600 tons.
