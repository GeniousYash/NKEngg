What is N.K. Engg. and what we do?

A short but Brief Info.

N.K. Engineering Dies And Tools, located in Manesar, Haryana, India, is a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-performance Plastic Injection Molding Machines.

Our product range is available in a variety of specifications and is offered at competitive prices to meet diverse requirements.

We conduct comprehensive testing on our machines using state-of-the-art tools to ensure flawless performance for our users.

Additionally, we offer Plastic Injection Molding Machine Repair Services to ensure ongoing satisfaction for our clients.

Clients value our products for their exceptional features, including superior quality, high performance, low maintenance, seamless functionality, ease of installation, and reliability.

We specialize in the manufacturing of molds and molded parts, offering comprehensive solutions for your plastic product needs. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped to produce medium-sized molds suitable for molding machines ranging from 50 tons to 550 tons, with a mold-making capacity of up to 600 tons.

With a robust operation running 2 shifts in our mold manufacturing department and 3 shifts in our molding department, we operate 15 advanced injection molding machines to ensure timely and efficient production.

Our core competencies include engineering tool design, tool manufacturing, operations, assembly, and precision injection molding, enabling us to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives our success. We strive to exceed your expectations and are ready to be your trusted partner for all your plastic product solutions.
